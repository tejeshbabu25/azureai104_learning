The AI-900 Microsoft Azure AI Fundamentals exam is designed for anyone who wants to understand the basics of Artificial Intelligence and how it is applied with Azure services. It covers key concepts in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and conversational AI. This exam is beginner-friendly and does not require prior technical experience, making it ideal for students, business professionals, or anyone curious about AI. Passing AI-900 validates your foundational knowledge and sets the stage for more advanced Azure AI certifications.
